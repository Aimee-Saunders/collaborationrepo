How Digital Entertainment has made Position Contributions to Society

Here are the main benefits:

Key Positive Contributions

Human Enjoyment

Humans love films! And games!

Creativity

The ease of making a video on your phone nowadays means that anyone can create entertainment for others.

Connection

People can watch and consume media from all over the world, and understand other cultures from it. They can then connect with people from across the world with an understanding of their culture.

Short Takeaways:

  1. Entertainment is key to human enjoyment.
  2. With the digital age, creating entertainment is easier than ever.
