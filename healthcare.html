Healthcare

This webpage demonstrates how computing has helped to improve healthcare

6 common uses for computers in healthcare

  • Medical and Patient Data
  • Medical inventory and tracking/managing supplies
  • Disease surveillance and management
  • Research
  • Surgical procedures
  • Communication and telemedicine

Medical and patient data

Medical records contain a wealth of information that can be used to improve the diagnosis and treatment of patients

This data is often scattered across different medical facilities and databases but IT can help bring this data together and make it more accessible

Medical inventory and tracking/managing

Medical inventory management tracks and manages the supplies and medications used in a health care setting. It helps to ensure that necessary supplies are always available when needed.

Thanks to computing, inventory can be monitored instantaneously, allowing for more efficient ordering of supplies

real-time inventory management helps to:

  • reduce costs
  • maintain stock levels
  • improve patient safety
  • optimize staff workflow
